Document E035UA-01
Branches Hub » UA-Hub » SCP-035-UA » Document E035UA-01
rating: +3+–x
Show experiment format
Сlose

All experiments should be written in the following format:

Name:
Date:
Terms:
Result:

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 07/19/2013
Terms: A white sheet of paper with the word "Hello" written on it.
Result: No reaction.

Note: Perhaps, the object responds only on texts with appropriate size? - Dr. R████

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 07/19/2013
Terms: 53 sheets of plain paper. On each was written the number of characters from 7 to 60.
Result: The effect began to appear from sheet #24 (30 characters). Original text was replaced on: "Why pray to Perun when Veles is here?"

Note: Well, now we know the minimum number of characters for the manifestation of the effect. - Dr. R████

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 07/19/2013
Terms: A sheet of plain paper with printed ████████ headphones instruction manual (12,000 characters).
Result: Description [DELETED] Slavic tribe's trade route, with the described goods, their quantity and equivalence. The number of characters matches the original.

Note: Forget about my theory that with the help of the object we communicate with the god Veles. However, we confirmed that the number of characters remains after the manifestation of the effect. - Dr. R████

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 07/21/2013
Terms: Parchment with the Ukrainian alphabet written on it (65 characters).
Result: A small prayer poem to the goddess Lada (65 characters).

Note: Effect manifests itself not only on paper. Let's try something more unsuitable for writing. - Dr. R████

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 07/21/2013
Terms: Metal plate with engraved Ukrainian alphabet (65 characters).
Result: The engraving disappeared, and list of names [DATA EXPUNGED] appeared on its place.

Note: Firstly, the effect acts on any texts, on any material. Secondly, the source text doesn't depend on the content of the original.
- Dr. R████

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 08/03/2013
Terms: Plain paper with handwritten English alphabet on it (52 characters).
Result: A small quote in English.

Note: Effect retains the original language, but texts still relate to the Slavs. - Dr. R████

Name: Dr. R████
Date: 08/10/2013
Terms: Text with 3567 characters on Ukrainian.
Result: Detailed description of [DATA EXPUNGED]. After seven (7) minutes, paper began to smolder and was completely destroyed after twenty-three (23) seconds. The content of the text was lost. Procedures for experiments were revised.

Note: According to the order of the Site Director, experiments with large texts should be carried out in the presence of at least two (2) personnel members with a third (3) access level and recorded by video surveillance.

doctor-r████oosupplementua
